How we build

Local first, human first

Skevia is a small studio betting on software that respects your machine, your attention, and your time—without dumbing things down.

  • Privacy by architecture Your private work stays on your own computer, not someone else’s servers—that starts with Zephra. Zephra on the products page
  • Clarity over jargon Vocari meets panic with plain language and next steps, because “error codes” shouldn’t be a literacy test. Vocari on the products page
  • Interfaces that get out of the way Luma hides the clutter in the programs you use every day so the screen feels calmer without a hunt through settings first. Luma on the products page
