How we build

Local first, human first

Skevia is a small studio betting on software that respects your machine, your attention, and your time—without dumbing things down.

  • Privacy by architecture Sensitive work stays on your hardware where it belongs—starting with Zephra’s fully local intelligence. Zephra on the products page
  • Clarity over jargon Vocari meets panic with plain language and next steps, because “error codes” shouldn’t be a literacy test. Vocari on the products page
  • Interfaces that get out of the way Luma trims noise from the tools you live in so the screen feels lighter by default, not after a dozen settings panels. Luma on the products page
