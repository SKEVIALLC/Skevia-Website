In the works

Studios, Veks AI, Zephra, Vocari, Luma, and Perea—local-first intelligence, clearer errors, and calmer interfaces, all written to feel legible and intentional.

Skevia Studios

Focused digital spaces for creative work, planning, and everyday momentum. Built to feel calm and clear instead of like another busy dashboard.

Veks AI

An AI layer that stays close to real workflows: useful, readable, and easy to follow. Meant to help without turning every task into a chat window.

Zephra

Zephra keeps your files organized and watches for trouble on your machine—fully local, no cloud required. It runs Llama 3.2 (1B) on your own hardware so nothing sensitive leaves the device.

Vocari

Vocari watches for system errors in the background and swaps technical jargon for plain English with simple next steps. It turns “what just happened?” into something you can actually fix.

Luma

Luma learns what you actually use in Word, Excel, Salesforce, and similar apps, then hides the rest of the UI noise. The same idea extends to the taskbar and Start menu so everyday computing feels lighter.

Perea

Perea is a new operating system meant to sit alongside Zephra, Vocari, Luma, and the rest of the Skevia line—security and performance as foundations, not afterthoughts. Built in assembly, C, and Rust so the stack stays explicit and under control where it counts.
